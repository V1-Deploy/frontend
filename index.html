How to find an Embark ID

To find a raider's Embark ID, look for the "Copy Embark ID" in the post-game report. The ID is then copied and can be pasted in the SEARCH bar.

Data Reporting

Integrity Protection

We actively take steps to protect against abuse including rate limiting, basic anomaly detection, and behavioral monitoring. We prioritize the privacy, safety, and trust of our users.
